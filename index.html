What is this?
Bullet Journaling is a methodology that requires urgency and persistent reflection. This self-destructing online list is used as an aid but not a replacement. When inspiration strikes and you don't have a bullet journal handy, this memo list is used as an inspiration bucket to jot down things on the go, but to prevent it from being a list that doesn't get used anywhere, the self-destruct nature requires the user to evaluate whether or not they want to add this item to their bullet journal within 48 hours, otherwise it will be lost forever ... or until it is added again.
